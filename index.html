Over the last couple of years, Calicut has emerged as one of the most rapidly developing cities in Kerala. The growth in infrastructure has led to a huge boom in the real estate market too. With more industries and educational institutions opening up, Kozhikode is one of the most sought after towns in Kerala by the builders to construct flats and villas. vista Developers, based out of Calicut is one of the most sought after builders in Kerala. One of the major business houses in Kerala, vista Group ventured into real estate in 1995.
